# 💸 GraceSoft Story Pricing Strategy

## 🧠 Core Positioning

GraceSoft Story is not just storage — it’s:

> **A structured timeline system for stories, projects, and progress**

So pricing should scale by:

* **Number of timelines**
* **Depth of features (ownership, collaboration, integrations)**
* **Automation & intelligence**

---

# 🟢 1. FREE — “Start Your Story”

**Target:** Individuals exploring / casual users
**Goal:** Remove friction, drive adoption

### Includes:

* ✅ 1 active timeline
* ✅ Basic story entries (text + images)
* ✅ Manual timeline creation
* ✅ Simple chronological view
* ✅ Public or private toggle
* ✅ Basic search

### Limitations:

* ❌ No integrations
* ❌ No collaboration
* ❌ No automation
* ❌ Limited storage (e.g. 100MB)

---

# 🔵 2. GROWTH — “Build Your Story”

**Target:** Creators, devs, indie builders (your core audience)

💡 *This is your MAIN conversion plan*

### Includes everything in Free +:

* ✅ Up to 10 timelines
* ✅ Rich story blocks (media, links, structured entries)
* ✅ Ownership & access control *(from your commits 👀)*
* ✅ Timeline editing & restructuring
* ✅ Version history (basic)
* ✅ GitHub integration *(manual refresh endpoint fits here perfectly)*
* ✅ Sync pipeline (basic)
* ✅ Private sharing via link/passphrase
* ✅ Increased storage (e.g. 5GB)

### Value Hook:

> “Turn your work into a structured, living story”

---

# 🟣 3. PRO — “Scale Your Story”

**Target:** Power users, teams, serious builders

### Includes everything in Growth +:

* ✅ Unlimited timelines
* ✅ Advanced ownership roles (team access)
* ✅ Real-time collaboration *(future-ready)*
* ✅ Advanced integrations (GitHub auto-sync, more sources later)
* ✅ Smart timeline automation *(based on commits / activity)*
* ✅ Milestone tracking & insights *(you already started this 👀)*
* ✅ Activity logs & audit trail
* ✅ Priority processing for sync pipelines
* ✅ Advanced privacy controls
* ✅ Large storage (e.g. 50GB+)

### Value Hook:

> “Let your work document itself”

---

# 🟡 Optional 4th Tier (Later) — TEAM / ORG

Only introduce when needed.

### Includes:

* Shared workspaces
* Team billing
* Admin dashboard
* Permissions matrix
* API access

---

Keep it simple + psychological:

* **Free** — $0

* **Pro** — **$19/month**

> 🎟 “50% off first month” → perfect for Growth
